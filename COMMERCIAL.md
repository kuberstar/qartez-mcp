# Commercial Licensing

Qartez is dual-licensed. The free tier (see [LICENSE](./LICENSE)) covers individuals and small teams that meet **both** of the following conditions:

- Your Company has three (3) or fewer Users who regularly interact with Qartez, measured over any rolling 90-day period.
- Your Company had less than $1,000,000 USD in gross commercial revenue in the prior fiscal year. Grants, donations, sponsorships, and crowdfunding contributions received in support of open-source work do not count toward that total. (First fiscal year of operation counts as zero revenue.)

You need a Commercial License if any of the following is true:

- Your Company has four (4) or more Users who regularly interact with Qartez, or
- Your Company had $1,000,000 USD or more in gross commercial revenue in the prior fiscal year, or
- You want to embed, bundle, or redistribute Qartez as a component of a paid product, hosted service, or consulting deliverable.

Users are counted by active seats, not headcount. A three-hundred-person company where only three people regularly use Qartez still counts as three Users (though it would likely exceed the revenue threshold).

## Getting a quote

Email **hello@qartez.dev** with:

- Your company name and website.
- Approximate number of Users who will interact with Qartez.
- A brief description of how Qartez will be used (internal tooling, bundled with a product, SaaS offering, consulting, client work).

Pricing is negotiated per-team and not published publicly. We usually reply within a few business days.

## Unsure whether you need a Commercial License?

Email hello@qartez.dev and describe your situation. We will answer directly, and if you qualify for the free tier we will say so, no quote required.
